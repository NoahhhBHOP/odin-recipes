How to Make an Egg

here is the ingredients to make your own egg at home

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon of water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Crack the egg into a bowl and add the tablespoon of water. Whisk together until well combined.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add a small amount of butter or oil to coat the bottom.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and let it cook for about 1-2 minutes, or until the edges start to set.
  4. Using a spatula, gently lift the edges of the egg and tilt the skillet to allow any uncooked egg to flow to the edges.
  5. Continue cooking until the egg is fully set but still slightly runny in the center.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Carefully slide the egg onto a plate and serve immediately.
  8. Enjoy your delicious homemade egg!
